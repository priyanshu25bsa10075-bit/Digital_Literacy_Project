## 🚀 **Project Overview**

As a *Student Digital Ambassador* at VIT Bhopal University, I have created this Digital Literacy Portfolio to help my peers navigate the digital world. This project involves the completion of five tasks that cover a range of essential digital skills, including online professional presence, communication etiquette, safe internet practices, coding platforms, and awareness of cybercrimes.

This portfolio contains all the work I have done in response to each task, ranging from building a professional online portfolio, practicing coding, learning proper email etiquette, and creating awareness materials about cybercrimes.

All files and resources related to this project are organized in the repository for easy access.

## 📝 **Project Tasks**

### **Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic**
- **Objective**: Design a one-page infographic to help peers understand digital literacy.
- **Tools Used**: Canva
- **Deliverables**: Infographic covering 3 topics: Digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

### **Task 2: Build Your Student Digital Portfolio**
- **Objective**: Set up accounts on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, Kaggle, Stack Overflow, and ResearchGate.
- **Tools Used**: GitHub, LinkedIn
- **Deliverables**: Screenshots of profiles and a brief description of the platforms.

### **Task 3: Explore Coding & Collaboration Platforms**
- **Objective**: Familiarize with coding practice platforms and collaboration tools.
- **Tools Used**: HackerRank, Google Forms
- **Deliverables**: Screenshot of completed coding challenge and Google Form quiz.

### **Task 4: Draft a Professional Email & Etiquette Guide**
- **Objective**: Demonstrate email etiquette and create a social media checklist.
- **Deliverables**: Two professional email drafts and a checklist for responsible social media use.

### **Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide**
- **Objective**: Research a cybercrime scenario and create a prevention guide.
- **Deliverables**: Case study on a cybercrime type and a checklist for prevention tips.
